About our company

Welcome to Exodus Companions, Your reliable partner for compassionate social connection.

At Exodus Companions, we're not just another company - we're your bridge to meaningful, joyful and lasting companionship. We understand that in today's fast-paced world, its easy to feel disconnected and lonely, but you don't have to go it alone. We're here to connect with people in a way that's exciting, enriching, and unforgettable.

Our commitment to providing compassionate services extends beyond mere assistance. We recognize the evolving needs of older individuals and aim to foster a sense of ease and independence in their own living spaces. Our comprehensive range of services is designed to accommodate varying levels of support, ensuring that each person receives personalized care tailored to their unique requirements.

    Our Objectives

  • To alleviate feelings of loneliness and isolation, and improve the social wellbeing of older people.
  • To build a sustainable system that will help older people to connect with their peers.
  • To help older people find lasting companionship and enjoy social interaction.
